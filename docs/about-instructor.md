# About Your Instructor

I'm Steve Wade. I rescue failed Kubernetes migrations.

50+ platform audits. Over £100M in complexity prevented. I'm the engineer organisations call when cloud-native promises become expensive nightmares.

I've been implementing Flux at scale since before it graduated from the CNCF. Former Flux maintainer. Trained 6,000+ engineers worldwide in cloud-native practices, 400+ specifically on GitOps.

My philosophy is simple: delete, don't add. Most platform teams are drowning in tools. I help them figure out which ones to remove.

## What I've seen

I've tracked 87 Kubernetes migrations. Teams starting with 5-8 tools had a 78% success rate. Teams starting with 25+ had 11%. Failed migrations cost 3x more than successful ones. Not just cloud spend. Engineer burnout, opportunity cost, and the consultant fees to fix it.

This workshop is built from those patterns. Not theory. Not vendor marketing. What actually works in production.
